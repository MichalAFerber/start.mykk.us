Features

Everything You Need,
Nothing You Don't

A fully-loaded start page that respects your privacy and works without an internet connection.

Single File

The entire dashboard is one HTML file. Download it, open it in your browser, and you're done. No install. No config.

Fully Customizable

Grid layout, gradient themes, custom backgrounds, widget sizing — every pixel is yours to control through an intuitive settings panel.

Drag & Drop Layout

Rearrange every section by grabbing its handle and dragging. Touch-friendly on mobile with always-visible drag handles.

Optional Cloud Sync

Works 100% offline with localStorage. Sign in with Google to sync settings across all your devices. Your choice.

Live Weather & Radar

Current conditions, 5-day forecast, and interactive radar with switchable layers. Animated overlays for rain, snow, and more.

Privacy First

All data stored locally in your browser. No tracking, no analytics, no accounts required. Open source so you can verify it yourself.

Getting Started

Up and Running in Seconds

Widgets

More Than Just Bookmarks

Built-in tools that turn your start page into a productivity hub.

Multi-Engine Search

Google, Bing, DuckDuckGo, Brave, Yahoo, Ecosia, and Startpage. Switch engines with a click.

Mini Calendar

Compact monthly calendar with navigation. Pairs with embeddable Google Calendar via iFrame widgets.

Paint Canvas

Full drawing tool with brush, shapes, text, and eraser. Export your creations as PNG or PDF.

Notepad

Quick notes that auto-save to your browser. Copy to clipboard or download as a text file anytime.

Favorites Bar

Drag-and-drop bookmarks with auto-detected favicons for 40+ popular services. Three icon style options.

iFrame Embeds

Embed anything — Google Calendar, RSS feeds, X/Twitter timelines, or any web content. Unlimited widgets.

Webcam & Selfie

Quick mirror view with a 3-second countdown timer, flash effect, and instant download. No app needed.

Clock & Date

Live clock with 12/24 hour format and multiple date styles. Configurable seconds display.
